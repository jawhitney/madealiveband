// Bio

Real life rock with a positive message

Made Alive is an original and cover band. We have a wide variety of rock songs in our set lists, bringing both rocking music and a positive message to all our fans. We hope to share the same optimistic views and great experiences we have with everyone else that comes to enjoy our live performances.

Made Alive's music is influenced by a wide variety of bands including Creed, Smashing Pumpkins, Def Leppard, Chevelle, Soundgarden, Evanescence, Breaking Benjamin, P.O.D., Fireflight and The Letter Black. The collaboration of our faith and personal journeys through lyrics laden with heavy guitar riffs and unique male vocals reaches to the core of anyone in search of something to relate to through music.
